Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What are the prices of services?

Prices vary but for home users they are:

  • Remote work starts from £35/hour
  • On-site work starts from £45/hour

For business users:

  • Remote work starts from £50/hour
  • On-site work starts from £65/hour

Factors that affect pricing include:

  • Type of service required
  • Complexity of the issue
  • Parts or software that may need to be purchased
What areas do you cover for on-site support?

I primarily cover the Sheffield area and surrounding cities for on-site support, including:

  • Worksop
  • Matlock
  • Mansfield
  • Staveley

If you are outside these areas, please contact me to discuss your requirements as I may still be able to assist you.

Do you offer remote support?

Yes, I offer remote support for a wide range of IT issues. Remote support is often quicker and more convenient for both home and business users, allowing me to diagnose and resolve problems without the need for an on-site visit.

What types of payment do you accept?

I accept various forms of payment including cash, bank transfer, and major credit/debit cards. Payment is typically due upon completion of the service unless otherwise agreed upon in advance.

What is your availability for appointments?

I typically offer appointments on weekdays, depending on my schedule. Please contact me to discuss your specific needs and availability.

Do you provide any guarantees on your work?

Yes, I stand by the quality of my work and offer guarantees on certain services. The specifics of the guarantee may vary depending on the type of service provided. Please discuss this with me when arranging your service.

How can I contact you for support?

You can contact me through the contact form on the Contact section of the website, or by calling the phone number provided there. I aim to respond to all inquiries as quickly as possible.

Do you offer any discounts for repeat customers?

Yes, I value my repeat customers and may offer discounts or special rates for ongoing support or multiple services. Please discuss this with me when arranging your service.

What should I do to prepare for an on-site visit?

To ensure a smooth on-site visit, please make sure that the area where the work will be performed is accessible and that any necessary equipment or information is available. It may also be helpful to back up any important data before the visit.
